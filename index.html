About Us

Women are like delicate glasses, each with its unique shape and strength. Just like glass, we can face shattering moments in life. Yet, it's in those moments of brokenness that we realize our resilience. Our support for each other is like the gentle hands that help assemble those pieces, reminding us of our collective strength and the beauty in our shared experiences.

Our Purpose

EmpowerHer exists as a beacon of hope and resilience for women who have experienced or are currently facing gender-based violence. We understand the importance of creating a safe space where women can connect, share their stories, seek advice, and uplift each other. Through mutual support and collective strength, we aim to break the silence surrounding gender-based violence and foster a community of empowerment and healing.

What We Offer

At EmpowerHer, we offer a platform for women to share their stories, seek advice, find support, and access resources to combat gender-based violence. Our community provides a safe and inclusive environment where women can connect, engage, and empower each other.
